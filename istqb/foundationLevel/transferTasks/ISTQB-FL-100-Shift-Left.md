# Shift Left Testing

## What is Shift Left?

### Definition 
**Shift Left Testing** means integrating testing activities **early and continuously** throughout the SDLC.

### Core Idea
- Testing is **not a phase**  
- Testing is **embedded in every stage**  

- Testing in every phase  
- Bugs are found early  
- Fixes are cheaper and faster  

---
##  Traditional vs Shift Left (Explanation)

###  Traditional Approach
Testing happens **after development is completed**.  
Defects are found **late**, when the system is already built.  

---

###  Shift Left Approach
Testing is **integrated early and continuously** in every phase  
(Requirements, Design, Development, CI/CD).  
Defects are found **as early as possible**.  

---

##  Why is it Important?
- Early defect detection  
- Lower cost of fixing defects  
- Reduced project risk  
- Faster feedback  

---

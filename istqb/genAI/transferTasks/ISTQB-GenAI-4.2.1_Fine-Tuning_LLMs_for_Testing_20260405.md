### 1. Title
Assess Fine-Tuning Feasibility for QA Use Cases

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 4.2.1 Fine-Tuning LLMs for Testing

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Determine when fine-tuning is justified compared with prompt engineering and retrieval approaches.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Stakeholders propose fine-tuning to improve test-case quality, but effort and governance implications are unclear.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Choose one QA use case with repeated AI quality issues.
2. Compare options:
   - Better prompting
   - RAG/context enrichment
   - Fine-tuning
3. Evaluate data needs, risk, cost, expected gain, and governance impact.
4. Create go/no-go criteria for fine-tuning.
5. Provide a recommendation with rationale.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Feasibility note with explicit recommendation and criteria.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Include data quality and labeling effort in cost estimation.
- Favor simpler options unless benefits are clear.

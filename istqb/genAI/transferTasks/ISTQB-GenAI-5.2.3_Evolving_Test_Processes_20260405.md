### 1. Title
Redesign a QA Process with Controlled GenAI Integration

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 5.2.3 Evolving Test Processes

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Integrate GenAI into an existing test process while preserving quality, traceability, and accountability.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team wants to modernize one core QA process using AI support without losing auditability.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Select one process (analysis, design, execution support, defect triage, or reporting).
2. Map current-state steps and pain points.
3. Define future-state process with clear AI-assisted and human-owned steps.
4. Add controls for quality checks, compliance, and traceability.
5. Assign ownership using a simple RACI model.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Updated process flow with controls and ownership model.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep human approval at key decision points.
- Avoid adding AI to steps with low value and high risk.

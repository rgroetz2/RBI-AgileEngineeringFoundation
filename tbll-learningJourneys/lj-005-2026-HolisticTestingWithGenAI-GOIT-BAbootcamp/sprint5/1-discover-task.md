# Hands-On Task  
## Validate Czech Market Expansion for Your Webshop

# Objective

The goal of this task is to validate whether expanding your webshop into the Czech market makes business sense.

You should NOT create a purely theoretical analysis.

Instead, collect real market evidence and answer your own business questions.

You define yourself:
- which products you analyze
- which competitors you investigate
- which marketplaces are relevant
- which risks you want to validate
- which opportunities you want to explore

## Learning Sprints
- The students are working in a learning circle. Meaning helping each other if necessary, or working together on a task.
- The learning is organized in a Scrum approach with weeks learning sprints. 
- Friday we are running a 1 hour meeting to discuss the progress and upcoming tasks

## Learning Tasks (LT)
- Every chapter/section of the Syllabus will be defined by the students as a learning task in the sprint backlog.

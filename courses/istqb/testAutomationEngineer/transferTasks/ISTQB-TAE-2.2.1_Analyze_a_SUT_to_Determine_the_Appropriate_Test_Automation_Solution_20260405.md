### 1. Title
Analyze Toolshop for Automation-Suitable Test Scope

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB TAE – 2.2.1 Analyze a System Under Test to Determine the Appropriate Test Automation Solution

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Assess SUT characteristics and derive an automation scope that balances value, risk, and feasibility.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team needs a targeted automation solution instead of trying to automate every test at once.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Explore Toolshop and list at least 8 observable SUT characteristics (UI dynamism, business-critical flows, data dependencies, third-party interactions).
2. Classify each characteristic as automation-friendly, automation-challenging, or neutral.
3. Choose a first-wave automation scope with exactly 5 tests.
4. Choose a deferred scope with exactly 3 tests and explain blockers.
5. Define one technical strategy for each blocker (e.g., stubs, selectors strategy, data seeding).

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
A short SUT analysis with first-wave scope, deferred scope, and technical strategy decisions.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
Business risk and repeatability usually outweigh “easy to script” as a prioritization factor.

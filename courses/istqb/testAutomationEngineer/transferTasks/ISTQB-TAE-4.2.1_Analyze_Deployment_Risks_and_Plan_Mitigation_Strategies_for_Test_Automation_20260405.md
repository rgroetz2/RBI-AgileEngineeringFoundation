### 1. Title
Build an Automation Deployment Risk Register for Toolshop

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB TAE – 4.2.1 Analyze Deployment Risks and Plan Mitigation Strategies for Test Automation

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Identify realistic deployment risks and define concrete mitigations for Toolshop automation.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
The team plans to enforce automation results in the pipeline and needs risk transparency before rollout.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. List 8 deployment risks (technical, process, and people-related).
2. Score each risk for probability and impact on a 1–5 scale.
3. Calculate risk exposure as probability × impact.
4. For the top 3 risks, define one mitigation action and one contingency action.
5. Assign owner and review frequency for each top risk.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
A ranked risk register with mitigation and contingency plans for the highest automation deployment risks.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
Include at least one flakiness-related risk and one environment-stability risk.

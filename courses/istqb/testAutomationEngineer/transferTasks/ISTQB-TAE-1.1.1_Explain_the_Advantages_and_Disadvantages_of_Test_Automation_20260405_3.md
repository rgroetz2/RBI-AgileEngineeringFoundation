### 1. Title
Build an Automation Decision Matrix for Toolshop Sprint 5

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB TAE – 1.1.1 Explain the Advantages and Disadvantages of Test Automation

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Evaluate where automation creates value and where it introduces risk for the Toolshop webshop. Produce a justified decision for specific candidate tests.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your Scrum team is preparing Sprint 6 planning for the Toolshop release from sprint5. The team asks you to recommend which regression checks should be automated next.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Open https://practicesoftwaretesting.com/ and explore these flows: product search, product detail view, cart update, and checkout start.
2. Review available product and sprint context in https://github.com/testsmith-io/practice-software-testing/tree/main/sprint5.
3. Select 4 test candidates (e.g., login, add-to-cart, filter, checkout field validation).
4. Create a decision matrix with columns: Frequency, Business Risk, Repeatability, Environment Stability, Setup Cost, Maintenance Cost.
5. Score each candidate from 1 (low) to 5 (high).
6. Mark each candidate as Automate Now, Automate Later, or Keep Manual, and add one short rationale.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
A completed decision matrix (table) with 4 candidates and a final recommendation list that clearly states automation advantages and disadvantages per candidate.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
Favor automation for high-frequency, stable, high-risk checks. Flag dynamic UI behavior and volatile data as potential disadvantages.

### 1. Title
Plan a Test Automation Pilot for Toolshop Sprint Delivery

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB TAE – 4.1.1 Apply Guidelines that Support Effective Test Automation Pilot and Deployment Activities

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Define practical pilot and rollout guidelines that reduce risk and maximize adoption of Toolshop automation.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your Scrum team wants to start automation quickly but lacks a structured pilot and deployment plan.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Select a pilot scope of exactly 3 Toolshop regression checks (e.g., login, search, add-to-cart).
2. Define pilot entry criteria and exit criteria.
3. Create a rollout plan with three phases: Pilot, Expansion, Operationalization.
4. For each phase, add owner, success metric, and one risk.
5. Add a Definition of Done for automated tests in your project context.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
A pilot-and-deployment plan including scope, criteria, phased rollout, and measurable success indicators.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
Keep pilot scope small enough to finish in one sprint and learn from concrete evidence.

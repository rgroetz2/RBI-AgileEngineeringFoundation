### 1. Title
Perform a Technical Health Check of Toolshop Automation

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB TAE – 8.1.2 Analyze the Technical Aspects of a Deployed Test Automation Solution and Provide Recommendations for Improvement

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Evaluate deployed automation from a technical perspective and formulate practical, prioritized recommendations.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Toolshop automation is deployed, but technical debt is growing and needs targeted improvement.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Assess the solution across dimensions: architecture, reliability, execution speed, diagnostics, and maintainability.
2. Rate each dimension on a 1–5 scale with one evidence statement.
3. Identify top 3 technical pain points.
4. Create recommendations in this format: action, expected benefit, effort estimate, owner.
5. Prioritize recommendations using High/Medium/Low business impact.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
A technical assessment sheet plus a prioritized recommendation list.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
Strong recommendations are specific and measurable, not generic statements.

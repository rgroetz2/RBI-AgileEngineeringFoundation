### 1. Title
Define and Apply Metrics for AI-Generated Test Artifacts

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 2.3.1 Metrics for Evaluating Generative AI Results

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Establish measurable criteria to evaluate quality of AI-generated outputs used in software testing.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team relies on AI-generated artifacts but lacks shared quality thresholds.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Define 5 metrics: correctness, completeness, clarity, traceability, actionability.
2. Create a scoring scale (e.g., 1–5) and acceptance threshold.
3. Evaluate one generated test artifact using the metric set.
4. Identify weakest dimension and propose one improvement action.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Metric framework plus one scored artifact example.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep scoring definitions explicit to reduce reviewer variance.
- Link low scores to concrete remediation steps.

### 1. Title
Build a Layered Mitigation Plan for GenAI Testing Risks

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 3.2.3 Mitigation Strategies

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Translate identified AI risks into an actionable and owned mitigation roadmap.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team has identified multiple AI-related risks but no concrete implementation plan.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Consolidate top 8 risks from prior analyses.
2. Define mitigation actions across:
   - Technical controls
   - Process controls
   - Human review controls
3. Assign owner and target date per action.
4. Define one KPI per control cluster.
5. Prioritize actions by risk reduction impact.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Risk mitigation roadmap with ownership and KPIs.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Focus on feasible sprint-level actions first.
- Link every mitigation to a concrete risk.

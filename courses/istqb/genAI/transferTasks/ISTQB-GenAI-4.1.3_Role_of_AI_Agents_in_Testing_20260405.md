### 1. Title
Define AI Agent Roles and Hand-offs in QA

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 4.1.3 Role of AI Agents in Testing

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Design a role-based AI agent model with clear responsibilities and human approval gates.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team considers multi-agent support for test analysis, design, and monitoring, but roles are unclear.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Define at least 3 agent roles (e.g., Analyst Agent, Design Agent, Monitoring Agent).
2. Specify input/output contract per role.
3. Define hand-off sequence and review checkpoints.
4. Identify one failure mode and fallback per role.
5. Add one governance rule for production usage.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Agent operating model with hand-off workflow.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep agent scope narrow and auditable.
- Never remove human accountability for release decisions.

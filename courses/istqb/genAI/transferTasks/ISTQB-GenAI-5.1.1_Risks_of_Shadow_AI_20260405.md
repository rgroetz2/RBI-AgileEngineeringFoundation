### 1. Title
Identify and Control Shadow AI Risks in QA Teams

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 5.1.1 Risks of Shadow AI

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Recognize Shadow AI patterns and define practical controls that balance enablement and compliance.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Some team members use unapproved AI tools for test work, creating quality and compliance risks.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. List 5 realistic Shadow AI scenarios in your testing context.
2. Assess impact on confidentiality, quality, and traceability.
3. Define preventive actions (approved tools, training, policy clarity).
4. Define detection/monitoring signals.
5. Create a lightweight escalation path.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Shadow AI control plan with prioritized actions.

---

### 7. Timebox
20 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Focus on realistic behavior, not only policy text.
- Include positive alternatives to discourage unsafe workarounds.

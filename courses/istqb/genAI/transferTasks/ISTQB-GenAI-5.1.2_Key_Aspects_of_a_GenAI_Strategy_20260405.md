### 1. Title
Create a Practical GenAI Strategy Canvas for Testing

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 5.1.2 Key Aspects of a GenAI Strategy

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Structure a pragmatic strategy linking GenAI vision, governance, use cases, and measurable outcomes for QA.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your organization wants a coherent GenAI direction for testing beyond isolated experiments.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Build a one-page strategy canvas with sections:
   - Vision and scope
   - Priority use cases
   - Governance and risk controls
   - Skills and enablement
   - Tooling and integration
   - Metrics and value tracking
2. Define 3 near-term and 3 mid-term goals.
3. Add prioritization criteria for use cases.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Completed strategy canvas for QA GenAI adoption.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep goals measurable and time-bound.
- Tie each use case to clear business/test value.

### 1. Title
Assess Privacy and Security Risk in Testing Prompts

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 3.2.1 Data Privacy and Security Risks

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Identify sensitive data exposure risks in AI prompt usage and define safe handling rules.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
The team uses real-like data in prompts. You must prevent accidental leakage of confidential or personal information.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Review 10 sample prompts used in your team.
2. Mark any sensitive content (personal, confidential, regulated, internal secrets).
3. Classify each risk by severity and likelihood.
4. Define redaction/masking rules and examples.
5. Create an “allowed vs prohibited” prompt-content checklist.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Prompt privacy/security risk register and safe-prompting checklist.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Include metadata sensitivity (IDs, keys, tokens), not just business text.
- Keep policy language unambiguous.

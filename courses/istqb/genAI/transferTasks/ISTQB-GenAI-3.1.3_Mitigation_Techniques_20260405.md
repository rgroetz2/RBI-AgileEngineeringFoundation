### 1. Title
Mitigate Low-Quality GenAI Output in Test Preparation

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 3.1.3 Mitigation Techniques

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Apply layered mitigations to reduce hallucinations, reasoning faults, and bias in AI-assisted QA work.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Recent AI outputs caused rework. Your team needs concrete mitigations at prompt, process, and governance levels.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Select one flawed AI-generated test artifact.
2. Define mitigations at three levels:
   - Prompt-level (constraints, structure, evidence requests)
   - Process-level (review, traceability, acceptance gates)
   - Governance-level (usage rules, escalation)
3. Re-run generation with mitigations in place.
4. Compare output quality before/after.
5. Document which mitigation had highest impact.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Mitigation table with measured before/after improvement.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep mitigations specific and testable.
- Avoid mitigation lists without ownership.

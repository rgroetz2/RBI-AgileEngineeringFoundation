### 1. Title
Identify Hallucinations, Reasoning Errors, and Bias in AI Test Outputs

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 3.1.2 Identifying Hallucinations, Reasoning Errors and Bias

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Systematically detect and classify quality issues in GenAI outputs before they enter test assets.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team receives AI-generated test ideas and wants a lightweight review checklist to detect major quality issues early.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Generate one AI output with at least 8 test ideas for a webshop flow.
2. Review the output with a checklist covering:
   - Unsupported claims
   - Logical contradictions
   - Missing preconditions
   - Biased assumptions
3. Flag at least 5 findings and classify severity (low/medium/high).
4. Validate each finding against system behavior/docs.
5. Produce corrected statements.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Checklist and reviewed output with evidence-backed findings.

---

### 7. Timebox
20 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Treat any claim without source evidence as suspect.
- Watch for absolute language (“always”, “never”).

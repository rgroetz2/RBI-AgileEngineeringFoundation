### 1. Title
Perform a GenAI QA Skills Gap Analysis

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 5.2.1 Required Skills and Knowledge

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Identify current vs required GenAI competencies per QA role and prioritize upskilling.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team has mixed GenAI knowledge levels and needs a role-based enablement plan.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Define required skills for tester, test lead, and automation engineer.
2. Assess current proficiency levels.
3. Identify top competency gaps.
4. Prioritize learning actions by impact and urgency.
5. Define one measurable target per role.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Skills matrix with prioritized development actions.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Include both technical and governance competencies.
- Keep proficiency scales simple and consistent.

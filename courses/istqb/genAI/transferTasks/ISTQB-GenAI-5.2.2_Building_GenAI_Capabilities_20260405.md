### 1. Title
Create a 90-Day GenAI Capability Build Plan for QA

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 5.2.2 Building GenAI Capabilities

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Plan coordinated capability growth across people, process, tools, and governance.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Leadership asks for a concrete short-term plan to make GenAI usage in testing reliable and scalable.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Define milestones for 30/60/90 days.
2. Include activities for:
   - Skills enablement
   - Process updates
   - Tooling setup
   - Governance controls
3. Assign owner per milestone.
4. Define success criteria and evidence.
5. Identify top 3 execution risks.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
90-day capability plan with ownership and milestones.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Prefer small, verifiable increments over broad commitments.
- Track adoption and quality outcomes together.

### 1. Title
Draft an LLMOps Process for QA Deployment and Control

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 4.2.2 LLMOps for Deployment and Management

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Define an operational process for safe deployment, monitoring, and lifecycle management of AI in testing.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your team needs a repeatable process to onboard, monitor, and update AI capabilities used in software testing.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Define lifecycle stages: onboarding, evaluation, release, monitoring, rollback, retirement.
2. Assign owner roles per stage.
3. Define release gates (quality, risk, compliance).
4. Define 5 KPIs for operational control.
5. Add incident handling and rollback trigger criteria.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
LLMOps blueprint with stages, owners, and KPIs.

---

### 7. Timebox
30 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep auditability and traceability mandatory.
- Define clear stop conditions for unsafe behavior.

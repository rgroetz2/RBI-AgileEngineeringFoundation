### 1. Title
Plan a Phased GenAI Adoption Roadmap for Testing

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 5.1.4 Phases of Adoption

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Define a phased rollout plan with explicit success criteria and controls for each stage.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your QA organization needs a controlled path from pilot to scaled GenAI usage.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Define phases: Pilot, Controlled Scale, Institutionalized Usage.
2. For each phase, specify:
   - Entry criteria
   - Exit criteria
   - Success metrics
   - Key risks and controls
3. Add timeline assumptions and dependencies.
4. Define decision gate owners.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Adoption roadmap with phase gates and metrics.

---

### 7. Timebox
25 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Keep gate criteria auditable and objective.
- Align phase goals with team capability maturity.

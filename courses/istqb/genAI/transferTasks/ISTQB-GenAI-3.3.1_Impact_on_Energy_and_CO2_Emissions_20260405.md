### 1. Title
Define Sustainable GenAI Usage Rules for Testing

---

### 2. Syllabus Reference
ISTQB GenAI – 3.3.1 Impact on Energy and CO₂ Emissions

---

### 3. Learning Objective
Recognize major drivers of AI energy use and define practical optimization rules for QA teams.

---

### 4. Context / Scenario
Your organization wants efficient AI usage without lowering testing quality.

---

### 5. Task Instructions
1. Identify cost drivers (prompt size, model choice, retries, unnecessary iterations).
2. Define 5 sustainable usage rules for your test team.
3. Estimate expected efficiency effect per rule (low/medium/high).
4. Add one quality safeguard per rule.
5. Propose one team KPI to monitor responsible usage.

---

### 6. Expected Outcome / Deliverable
Sustainable prompting guideline with quality safeguards.

---

### 7. Timebox
20 minutes

---

### 8. Hints (Optional)
- Optimize prompt quality before increasing model size.
- Reuse validated prompt templates to reduce retries.

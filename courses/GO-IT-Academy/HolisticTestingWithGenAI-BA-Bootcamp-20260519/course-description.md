# Holistic Testing with GenAI for Business Analysts

This hands-on training introduces Business Analysts to the Holistic Testing Model and demonstrates how Generative AI can support quality engineering activities across the entire delivery lifecycle.

Participants work through all 8 stages (Discover, Plan, Understand, Build, Deploy, Release, Observe, Learn) of the Holistic Testing Model and apply practical testing techniques, methods, and approaches in collaborative workshops and hands-on exercises.

A central focus of the training is Exploratory Testing. Participants learn how to:

- Create test ideas and test charters
- Execute Session-Based Exploratory Testing (SBTM)
- Run exploratory sessions in single, pair, and mob formats
- Apply risk-based and quality-oriented thinking throughout the lifecycle

Generative AI is used to support activities such as:

- Risk analysis
- Test idea generation
- Requirement reviews
- Test design
- Exploratory charter creation

Examples of practical exercises include:

- Risk-Based Test Planning
- Acceptance criteria reviews
- Exploratory testing workshops
- Test design techniques

A major objective of the training is to transform the created testing artifacts and insights into a practical Test Automation Strategy for the system under test.

Participants learn how:

- Risks influence automation priorities
- Exploratory findings support automation decisions
- Business processes shape automation scope
- Different testing types require different automation approaches
- GenAI can support automation planning and strategy design

The final outcome of the training is a collaboratively created Test Automation Strategy including:

- Automation scope and priorities
- Risks and mitigation approaches
- Test levels and testing types
- Tooling and CI/CD considerations

The training is highly interactive, workshop-oriented, and focused on practical application in real project environments.

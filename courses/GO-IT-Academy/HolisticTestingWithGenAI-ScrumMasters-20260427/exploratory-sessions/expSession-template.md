# Exploratory Session Sheet Template

## CHARTER
Describe the mission of this exploratory session in 1-2 sentences.
State the feature or workflow to explore and the main quality concerns.

Example:
Analyze [feature/workflow] and report on defects that impact [business or user value].

## TESTER
Enter the name of the tester who executed the session.

Example:
FirstName LastName

## TEST NOTES
Summarize what you explored during the session, including focus areas and strategy.

Example:
I explored [scope], focusing on [key behaviors, edge cases, or misuse paths].

### View:
List screens, components, or system areas you visited.

Example:
- Page or component 1
- Page or component 2
- Page or component 3

### Use Cases:
List concrete scenarios you executed, including normal and edge cases.

Example:
- Happy path scenario
- Negative scenario
- Boundary value scenario

## RISKS
Document product, technical, or user-facing risks discovered during exploration.
Focus on why each risk matters.

Example:
- Risk statement 1
- Risk statement 2
- Risk statement 3

## BUGS
Document confirmed defects found in the session.
Use one entry per defect with a unique identifier and clear behavior.

Example:
BUG TS-0001
Short defect title or summary.

BUG TS-0002
Short defect title or summary.

## Summary
Provide a concise overall assessment and release readiness signal.
Mention whether follow-up testing or fixes are needed.

Example:
Core flow works with limitations; major defects in [area] should be fixed before release.

# E1.4: ESG Mission Statement Integration
As a sustainability-conscious customer
I want to see the company's ESG commitment clearly stated on the website
So that I can understand environmental and social values before purchasing

## Acceptance Criteria:
- Dedicated "Sustainability" section in main navigation
- ESG mission statement prominently displayed on About Us page
- Footer links to sustainability page from all site pages
- Legal page includes ESG compliance statements
- Company vision statement updated to include sustainability goals
- ESG policy downloadable as PDF for B2B customers
- Mobile-responsive display of ESG content

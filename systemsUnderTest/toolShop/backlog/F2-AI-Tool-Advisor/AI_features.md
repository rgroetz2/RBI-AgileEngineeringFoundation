# F1: Product Reviews & Ratings
Product Reviews & Ratings transforms Roy's Roy's from a transactional catalog into a trust-based community marketplace.
By enabling verified buyers to share authentic experiences, we eliminate purchase anxiety and replace manufacturer 
marketing with genuine user feedback. This builds the trust essential for confident purchasing decisions, particularly 
for professionals making bulk investments. Beyond individual transactions, it creates an engaged community where 
customers become contributors, establishing our competitive advantage through collective wisdom and loyalty.
## Goal
Build customer trust and transparency
Ratings visible on product pages; average rating calculated; users can leave feedback

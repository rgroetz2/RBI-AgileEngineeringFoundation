As a business analyst,

# Acceptance Criteria
- System supports different price configurations per region.
- Prices can be adjusted based on market conditions.
- Regional pricing rules are configurable in the admin interface.
- Changes to pricing strategies are logged for audit purposes.
- Pricing updates are reflected across the storefront within minutes.

# US-1008: Remove Product Group Rentals

As the CSO, 
I want to remove the "Rentals" product group from the web-shop 
to streamline the product offerings and focus on core merchandise.

# Acceptance Criteria:





- Review the current product catalog to identify all products belonging to the "Rentals" product group.
- Update the web-shop interface to remove any references or links to the "Rentals" product group from navigation menus, category pages, and search results.
- Ensure that users can no longer browse or access products categorized under the "Rentals" product group through the web-shop interface.
- Verify that the "Rentals" product group is no longer visible or accessible in the web-shop interface.
- Test the functionality of the web-shop to ensure that removing the "Rentals" product group does not impact the usability or performance of the platform.
- Confirm with the product owner that the desired changes have been successfully implemented and that the "Rentals" product group is effectively removed from the web-shop.


# US-1013 Open Card POC
As the product owner, 
I want to conduct a proof of concept (POC) with the OpenCart solution 
to evaluate its suitability as an alternative to our self-developed e-commerce platform.

# Acceptance Criteria:
- Research the features and capabilities of the OpenCart solution to determine its suitability for our e-commerce needs.
- Evaluate the ease of installation, customization options, available extensions, and overall usability of OpenCart compared to our self-developed e-commerce platform.

Feature Comparison:
- Identify key features and functionalities of our self-developed e-commerce platform.
- Compare these features with the equivalent functionalities available in OpenCart, assessing their similarity, usability, and customization options.
- Analyze the results of the POC and compare the benefits and drawbacks of OpenCart versus our self-developed platform.
- Make an informed decision based on the POC findings, considering factors such as cost, effort, scalability, and long-term maintainability.

# US-F4.1-prices-in-usd
As a US customer, 
I want to see all prices in USD 
so I understand costs without conversion.

# Acceptance Criteria
- All product prices are displayed in USD on product listing and detail pages.
- Currency symbol "$" appears before the price.
- Currency selection is automatically set to USD for users detected in the US.
- Prices remain consistent across cart and checkout pages.
- Currency format follows US standards (e.g., $1,299.00).

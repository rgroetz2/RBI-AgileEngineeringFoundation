# US-F4.4-shipping-cost-visibility
As a US customer,
I want to see shipping costs before checkout
so there are no surprises.

# Acceptance Criteria
- Shipping costs are displayed on the product page or cart page.
- Estimated shipping cost updates based on the customer's location.
- Free shipping limits are clearly shown if they exist.
- Shipping cost appears in the order summary before payment.
- If shipping cost cannot be calculated yet, a clear estimate is shown.

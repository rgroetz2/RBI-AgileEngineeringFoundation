# US-1007 New Logo
As CMO, I want to create a new logo for the e-commerce platform 
to enhance the platform's brand identity and visual appeal.

# Acceptance Criteria:

Gathering Requirements and Inspiration:
- Conduct a meeting with the product owner to gather requirements and preferences for the new logo design.
- Research industry trends and competitor logos to gather inspiration and ensure that the new logo aligns with current design standards and market expectations.

Design and Iteration Process:
- Create multiple logo design concepts based on the gathered requirements and inspiration.
- Present the initial design concepts to the product owner for feedback and review.

Finalization and Delivery:
- Finalize the logo design based on the product owner's approved concept and feedback.
- Ensure that the final logo design is scalable and suitable for use across various platforms and marketing materials.
- Deliver the final logo files to the product owner in the required formats (e.g., PNG, SVG, EPS) for immediate use in branding and promotional materials.
- Update the source code and make the new logo available.
